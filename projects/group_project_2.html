Research Strategy

Library Research

Initial research done by literature study; searching through the internet about the city of Breda, and what interesting and appealing things Breda have. Moreover, research on the marketing aspects, general digital and traditional marketing strategies, and the marketing strategy of the city of Breda itself.

Field Research

This type of research will be where we collect the hypothesis for the creation of our media products. The types of research we will do in this category include:

  • Survey: We did a survey that is targeted at our target group, to understand their interests and needs, then analyzed the result to improve our media product creations.
  • Stakeholder analysis: We did this research by maintaining regular communication with the stakeholders to see if our creations meet the needs of the stakeholders.

Lab Research

Throughout the iterative media products creation process, we did several user testings after the products reach a certain stage of development. This research will be done to further analyze the interest of our target audience and improve the products to be closer to what the user needs.

Workshop Research

This research was be done by prototyping the media products we plan to make: flyer, poster, digital poster, cinematic video and trendy video for social media advertisement. These prototypes will serve as proof of concept to support our marketing strategy.
